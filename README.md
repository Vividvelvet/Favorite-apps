# Useful Apps

A collection of my favorite Android apps and tools organized by category.

## 📊 Tracking and Digital Health

- [**Track and Graph**](https://github.com/SamAmco/track-and-graph) – Tracking behaviours and feelings
- [**ActivityWatch**](https://github.com/ActivityWatch/activitywatch) – Tracks phone use
- [**Screen Time**](https://github.com/markusfisch/ScreenTime) – Tracks phone use
- [**Just Delete Me**](https://github.com/jdm-contrib/jdm) – Provides instructions for deleting personal data from a large variety of platforms
- [**Batch Uninstaller**](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.simplicity.shortcutter) – Allows mass deleting of apps
- [**Canta**](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=app.simple.canta) – Delete any app without root
- [**Digipaws**](https://github.com/nethical6/digipaws) – Creates focus mode sessions for blocking apps
- [**Reef**](https://github.com/aload0/Reef) – Creates focus mode sessions for blocking apps
- [**NotiFilter**](https://github.com/BURG3R5/notifilter) – Blocks certain notifications by regex
- [**Native Alpha**](https://github.com/cylonid/NativeAlphaForAndroid) – Turns websites into apps
- [**Mindful**](https://github.com/akaMrNagar/Mindful) – Sets limits on phone and tracks behaviour

## 🎨 Creative

- [**Open Camera**](https://github.com/AntumDeluge/Open_Camera) – Great camera app, features an overlay function
- [**Image Toolbox**](https://github.com/T8RIN/ImageToolbox) – Post processing tools for images
- [**FadCam**](https://github.com/anonfaded/FadCam) – Recording screen off
- **CameraOverlay by RiseFall** – (NOT OPEN SOURCE)

## 📅 Contacts and Calendar

- [**Birthday Adapter**](https://github.com/SufficientlySecure/birthday-calendar) – Syncs birthdays to calendar
- [**Fossify Calendar**](https://github.com/FossifyOrg/Calendar) – Calendar App syncs with CalDAV
- [**Davx5**](https://github.com/bitfireAT/davx5-ose) – Syncs calendar and contacts

## 📱 System Management & Downloads

- [**Shizuku**](https://github.com/RikkaApps/Shizuku) – Remove apps without root
- [**ShizuTools**](https://github.com/legendsayantan/ShizuTools) – Expansion of Shizuku
- [**LibreTorrent**](https://github.com/proninyaroslav/libretorrent) – Android Torrent app (Great results!)
- [**Seeker**](https://github.com/jackBonadies/SeekerAndroid) – Soulseek for Android
- [**Seal**](https://github.com/JunkFood02/Seal) – Video downloader

## 🌙 Wellness & Relaxation

- [**Noice**](https://github.com/trynoice/android-app) – Calming noises for sleep

## 🌐 Privacy & Networking

- [**Tor Browser**](https://github.com/TheTorProject/gettorbrowser) – Private Browser on the Tor network

## 🍔 Health & Nutrition

- [**FoodYou**](https://github.com/maksimowiczm/FoodYou) – Local calorie tracker

## 🎨 Customization

- [**Peristyle**](https://github.com/Hamza417/Peristyle) – Multi wallpaper manager

## 💬 Messaging

- [**Element X**](https://github.com/element-hq/element-x-android) – Matrix messaging app

## ⌨️ Alternative Keyboards

- [**AnySoftKeyboard**](https://github.com/AnySoftKeyboard/AnySoftKeyboard) – Customizable open-source keyboard
- [**HeliBoard**](https://github.com/HeliBorg/HeliBoard) – Privacy-conscious customizable keyboard
- [**UnexpectedKeyboard**](https://github.com/Julow/Unexpected-Keyboard) – Lightweight keyboard with swipe support
- [**FlorisBoard**](https://github.com/florisboard/florisboard) – Modern open-source keyboard

## 🎵 Music & Audio

- [**Recording Studio Lite**](https://github.com/Leonidius20/RecordingStudio) – Basic recording
- [**Noten Lernen**](https://github.com/MelvilQ/noten-lernen) – Learn music notes
- [**Musekit**](https://github.com/Kwasow/Musekit) – Simple tuner and metronome
- [**Hexpress**](https://github.com/jmiskovic/hexpress) – Musical instruments on your phone (fun but lots of latency issues, especially with headphones)
- [**Helio**](https://github.com/helio-fm/helio-sequencer) – Musical sequencer/piano roll on phone
- [**Chronal**](https://github.com/cognitivitydev/Chronal) – Metronome and tuner with extra features
- [**ChordReader 2**](https://github.com/AndInTheClouds/chordreader2) – Find and store chords for songs
